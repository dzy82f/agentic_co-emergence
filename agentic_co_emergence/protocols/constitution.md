# Tychevia Constitution

Core rule:
- The Runtime governs process.
- The Circle contributes reasoning.
- No agent may synthesise before synthesis is unlocked.
- Review is separate from discussion.
- Governance, reasoning, knowledge, memory and semantic graph records must remain distinct.

Formal Organisation:
- Owns review, interpretation and future modification of protocols.

Circle:
- Owns discussion during DISCUSSION state only.
